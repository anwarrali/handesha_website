Who We Are

About Our Club

Handesha Sah is an engineering club at Al-Quds University under the Faculty of Najjad Zeeni Faculty of Engineering, founded and led by students from different disciplines who share a common goal: closing the gap between what we study and what the real world demands. We focus on practical exposure through workshops, technical sessions, competitions, and hands-on initiatives that push students to apply their knowledge beyond the classroom. Handesha Sah creates a collaborative and energetic environment where ideas are tested, skills are developed, and teamwork is valued. We aim to build a community that encourages growth, initiative, and meaningful engagement with the engineering field while keeping the spirit of student life at its core.
